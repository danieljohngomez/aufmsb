2.3.5 Changelog

AUF Mobile SchoolBliz is now recognized by Angeles University Foundation as an official app of its SchoolBliz system. Future changes are deployed using AUF's developer account.

Part of the officialization are these changes:

  • - Improved stability
  • - Push notifications
  • - Separation of schedule and dashboard
  • - Remember me feature
  • - Bugfixes
